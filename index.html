About

We are a subgroup of a student club (HackUSU) at Utah State University that is laser focused on tech.

Our Mission

HackUSU is bringing hacker culture to our campus, community, and state.

Our Hackers

  • Learn how to code - No experience required
  • Teach each other
  • Learn about new and emerging technologies
  • Meet awesome mentors in the community
  • Eat free food
  • Become lifelong learners

When and where?

The hackathon will be located at the Eccles Conference Center on the Utah State University Logan campus. It will begin at 9 am on Friday, November 17th and end at 9pm on Saturday, November 18th.

FAQ

What is hacking?
No, we aren't breaking into anything. Hacking is a new process of learning. To us, hacking means creating, innovating, and building something awesome with software or hardware. Another definition that we have is creatively solving a problem.
I can't code. Can I still attend?
Absolutely! If you have a passion for all things tech, this is the place for you. Our events span a range of skill levels. We have events planned for beginners, so you'll learn how to code in no time!
How much does it cost?
HackUSU is completely free! There's no entry fee, and we'll provide enough food and snacks for the entire 36 hours. How neat is that?
How many people can be on my team?
Teams can have up to 4 people. If you don't have a team when you come, don't worry! There are plenty of people who come alone that you can team up with.
Who can come to HackUSU?
You must currently be a university or college student, or have graduated in the last 12 months. There is no restriction on major. All students are welcome!
I don't really want to code. Is there something else I can do at HackUSU?
Absolutely! Many times, coming up with an idea for a project is the hardest part, and that doesn't require any technical knowledge. Also, we need designers to help us make our apps look good!
Can I start now?
You can't use any code that was written before the start of the hackathon. However, we encourage you to begin thinking of ideas now!
Will there be prizes?
Yes, there will definitely be prizes! We will have different prize categories, and the judges will pick the best project in each category. In addition, there will be lots of swag from our sponsors for everyone!
What are some of the prize categories?
This year we will have a special "Hack the Homeless" prize category. One of our sponsors, Utah Open Data Catalog, has millions of rows of data, and we want to use it to help the homeless! We will also have categories such as "Best Game" and "Best Hardware Hack".
