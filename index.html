FAQ

What is hacking?
No, we aren't breaking into anything. Hacking is a new process of learning. To us hacking means creating, innovating, and building something awesome with software or hardware. Another definition that we have is creatively solving a problem.
I can't code, can I still attend?
Absolutely! If you have a passion for all things tech, this is the place for you. Our events have a range of skill levels and we have have events planned around beginners so you'll learn how to code in no time!
How much does it cost?
HackUSU is completely free! No entry fee, and we'll provide enough food and snacks for the entire 36 hours. How neat is that?
